# 📑 Project Summary – Global GDP Analysis (2020–2025)

## 🔹 Overview
This project analyzes the **GDP growth trends of 196 countries from 2020–2025**.  
It focuses on **India’s performance vs other Asian economies**, the **Top 10 largest economies by 2025**, and the **fastest-growing countries worldwide**.  

The analysis highlights **shifting global economic power**, emerging markets’ rise, and India’s strong growth trajectory.

---

## 🔹 Key Insights
- 🇮🇳 **India’s CAGR (2020–2025): ~8.1%** → one of the highest among major economies.  
- 🌍 **Top 10 Countries by GDP (2025):** USA, China, India, Japan, Germany, UK, France, Brazil, Canada, Italy.  
- ⚡ **Fastest-Growing Economies:** India, Vietnam, Bangladesh, Philippines, Ethiopia – strong emerging market momentum.  
- 📉 Developed economies show **slower growth**, while Asia & Africa drive global expansion.
